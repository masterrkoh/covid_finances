# covid_finances
An analysis into what the after effect of the pandemic on the stock market.

From the research I was able to find out which sectors recovered fastest. I was also able to see which sectors crashed first, why they crashed earlier and why they took longer to recover.

The first sectors to crash were Energy and Consumer discretionary. Energy due to the fact that Russia and Saudi Arabia were at a price war. Consumer discretionary was due to the pandemic, people were forced to stay home and were unable to go out but it also recovered quite fast as people were allowed back out. With Health recovering fastest because more people were seeking treatment and care. Tech recovered second fastest as remote work became the norm.Remote work, video calls, streaming, online shopping, cloud computing. Every aspect of daily life moved online overnight and tech companies were the direct beneficiaries. Finance also took a long time to recover as they struggle to repay loans, investment activity drops, and banks became cautious. The financial system is deeply tied to economic activity so it takes longer to recover confidence.
